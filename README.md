# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 13 June 2026

KupaKur FC26 is a free tournament organizer app designed for small gaming groups, friends, office teams, and invited tournament participants who want to organize and follow local FC26 PlayStation tournaments.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect the following information:

- Player name entered during tournament registration
- Team preference entered during tournament registration
- Tournament comments and replies entered by users, where available
- Device-specific notification token used to deliver tournament-related push notifications
- Basic tournament interaction data such as likes, dislikes, registrations, scores, fixtures, standings, and tournament participation information

## How We Use Information

The information is used only to provide the app’s tournament features, including:

- Creating and managing tournaments
- Managing tournament registrations
- Creating tournament fixtures
- Displaying player lists, scores, standings, knockout brackets, and tournament news
- Sending tournament-related push notifications for private or organizer-managed tournaments
- Syncing tournament data between devices where online features are used
- Maintaining tournament history and archive information for organizer-managed tournaments

## Public and Private Tournament Data

Public local tournaments may be stored on the user’s device. Private or organizer-managed tournaments may use Firebase services to sync tournament data, registrations, scores, news, notifications, comments, and archive information.

Players may access private tournaments using an invite code provided by the tournament organizer. Invite-code access allows players to follow the tournament and register when registration is open. It does not provide admin access.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Cloud Messaging, to support admin login, data synchronization, and push notifications.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data is not used for advertising or tracking.

## Data Retention

Tournament data may remain available in the app for tournament history, archive, and app functionality purposes. Users may contact the app administrator to request correction or deletion of their submitted registration or comment information.

## Children

The app is not specifically directed to children. It does not contain gambling, betting, paid contests, real-money prizes, advertising, or paid content.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the app administrator.
